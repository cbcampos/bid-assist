# BidAssist Feature Wishlist

## Priority 1: Core Business Features

### 1.1 Company Profile Settings
- **Logo Upload** — PNG/JPG company logo for proposals
- **Company Info** — Name, address, phone, email, website
- **License Numbers** — AL contractor license #, insurance certs
- **Tagline** — Custom tagline on all outputs
- **Color Scheme** — Pick brand colors for proposals

### 1.2 Client Management (Enhanced)
- **Full Contact History** — All emails, calls, meetings
- **Job History** — Every project, bid, invoice linked to client
- **Communication Log** — Notes from every interaction
- **Preferred Pricing Tier** — Gold/Silver/Bronze client rates
- **Credit Terms** — Net 15/30/60 payment terms per client
- **Documents Storage** — Upload contracts, PDFs per client

### 1.3 Proposal/Estimate Builder (Enhanced)
- **Line Item Library** — Pre-built items with costs
- **Phase/Category Grouping** — Group items by work phase
- **Markup Calculator** — Apply % markup to labor/material
- **Tax Calculation** — Auto-add Alabama sales tax (9%)
- **Discount Field** — Percentage or fixed discount
- **Terms & Conditions** — Custom legal text
- **Proposal Templates** — Save/load proposal formats
- **PDF Export** — Professional PDF generation

---

## Priority 2: Operational Features

### 2.1 Project Tracking
- **Project Dashboard** — All active jobs at a glance
- **Gantt Chart View** — Visual timeline of all projects
- **Milestone Tracking** — Permits, dig, backfill, final grade
- **Photo Documentation** — Upload job site photos
- **Issue Log** — Track problems and resolutions
- **Client Approvals** — Digital sign-off on phases

### 2.2 Subcontractor Management (Enhanced)
- **Trade Specialties** — Tag subs by what they do
- **Availability Calendar** — When each sub is booked
- **Insurance Tracking** — Expiration dates for liability
- **Rating System** — 1-5 stars based on past work
- **Quote Requests** — Send RFQs to multiple subs
- **Contract Storage** — Keep sub agreements on file

### 2.3 Equipment Fleet (Enhanced)
- **Maintenance Log** — Every repair, oil change, service
- **Hour Tracking** — Link to hour meters
- **Fuel Log** — Gallons, cost, date
- **Utilization Report** — % time working vs idle
- **Assignment Log** — Which job each piece is on
- **Service Reminders** — Auto-alert at set hours

### 2.4 Field Reports (Enhanced)
- **Crew List** — Who worked, hours each
- **Equipment Used** — Which machines on site
- **Materials Delivered** — What arrived, quantities
- **Weather Conditions** — Auto-captured or manual
- **Issues/Delays** — What went wrong
- **Next-Day Plan** — Crew's plan for tomorrow
- **Client Sign-off** — Digital signature on completion

## Priority 3: Communication Features

### 3.1 Client Portal (Enhanced)
- **Live Project Status** — Client sees current phase
- **Progress Photos** — Uploaded daily for client view
- **Approval Requests** — Client approves change orders
- **Invoice Viewing** — Pay online link (Stripe integration)

- **Quote Expiration** — "Your quote expires in 7 days"
- **Invoice Reminders** — Past due notifications

### 3.3 SMS/Text Notifications
- **Job Starting** — "We're on our way!"

- **Appointment Reminder** — "See you tomorrow at 9am"

## Priority 4: AI-Powered Features

### 4.1 AI Estimating Assistant
- **Description Generator** — AI writes scope descriptions
- **Quantity Estimator** — AI estimates quantities from photos
- **Risk Detector** — AI flags potential scope gaps
- **Competitor Comparison** — Paste competitor bid → AI analysis

### 4.2 AI Scheduling
- **Weather Integration** — Auto-schedule around rain
- **Crew Availability** — Smart scheduling based on who's free
- **Travel Time** — Optimized route between jobs
- **Sub Matching** — Match job type to available subs

### 4.3 AI Voice Reports
- **Speech-to-Report** — Voice memo → structured report
- **Auto-Categorization** — AI sorts line items
- **Cost Extraction** — AI reads quantities from description

### 4.4 AI Document Scanner
- **Plan Reading** — Upload PDF → AI extracts measurements
- **Contract Analysis** — Flag unusual terms in contracts
- **Invoice Data Entry** — Photo receipt → auto-fill fields

### 5.2 Job Costing (Enhanced)
